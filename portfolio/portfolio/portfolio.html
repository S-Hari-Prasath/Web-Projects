PROJECT 1

WATER ALERT SYSTEM

Read More

Our proposal involves a water alert system that notifies users when water is supplied to a specific location via SMS or call, based on their preferences. Water sensors installed in strategic areas, such as shared tank pipelines, detect water flow and send data to a microcontroller. The microcontroller transmits this data to a GSM module, which sends an alert to the registered user's mobile number. Users can register on a website or at a local browsing center, providing their phone number, location, and preferred alert method (SMS or call).
